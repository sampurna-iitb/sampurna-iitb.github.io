Because “you'll figure it out” is not a plan.

Sampurna is a alumni(and current students)-led initiative born from lived experience at IIT Bombay. We create thoughtful, practical programs that help students navigate college more confidently—especially the parts that aren't taught in classrooms. Sometimes that means telling the quieter stories of IITB through initiatives like Before We Go, so students feel seen beyond the “machau” narrative. Sometimes it means building hands-on skill pathways like COMFORT, so students learn how to teach themselves technology and work effectively. What stays constant is our focus: making college life feel a little more human, a little more structured, and a lot more doable.
